Norwegian Traditions

National Costume (Bunad)

The bunad is Norway's traditional costume, worn during celebrations like Constitution Day (May 17th). Each region has its unique design reflecting local history and craftsmanship.

Learn about regional variations →

Christmas Traditions

Norwegian Christmas includes festive markets, gingerbread baking, and the Julebord feast. Families light advent candles and exchange gifts on Christmas Eve.

Discover festive recipes →

Midsummer Festival

Celebrated with bonfires and dances, Midsummer (Sankthansaften) marks the longest day of the year, filled with folklore and community gatherings.

Norwegian Cuisine

From hearty seafood dishes to sweet pastries, Norwegian food reflects its coastal and rural heritage.

Smoked Salmon

A staple of Norwegian cuisine, often served with dill and rye bread.

Lutefisk

A traditional dish made from dried cod, soaked in lye, popular during winter holidays.
