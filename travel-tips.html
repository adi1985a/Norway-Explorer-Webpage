Weather Information

Current Weather

Seasonal Weather Tips

Spring (March-May)

Temperature: 0°C to 15°C

What to expect: Mild temperatures, occasional rain, blooming landscapes

Summer (June-August)

Temperature: 12°C to 25°C

What to expect: Midnight sun, warm days, ideal for outdoor activities

Autumn (September-November)

Temperature: 5°C to 15°C

What to expect: Beautiful fall colors, increasing rainfall

Winter (December-February)

Temperature: -10°C to 5°C

What to expect: Snow, northern lights, winter sports opportunities

Visa Requirements

Do I Need a Visa?

Norway is part of the Schengen Area. Here's what you need to know:

  • EU/EEA citizens: No visa required
  • US citizens: No visa for stays up to 90 days
  • Other nationalities: Check the Norwegian Directorate of Immigration

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (at least 6 months beyond stay)
  • Travel insurance
  • Return ticket
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Sufficient funds for your stay

Safety Information

Emergency Numbers

  • Police: 112
  • Ambulance: 113
  • Fire: 110
  • Search & Rescue: 120

Safety Tips

  • Norway is generally very safe
  • Take precautions in remote areas
  • Check weather conditions before hiking
  • Carry appropriate gear for outdoor activities
  • Follow local guidelines and warnings

Transportation Guide

Public Transportation

Trains

  • Operated by NSB (Norwegian State Railways)
  • Extensive network connecting major cities
  • Scenic routes available (Bergen & Flåm Railway)
  • Book tickets via vy.no
  • Minipris tickets for best deals

Buses

  • Extensive local & long-distance networks
  • NOR-WAY Bussekspress for intercity travel
  • Local apps for real-time schedules
  • City passes available in major cities
  • Night buses in larger cities

Metro & Tram

  • Available in Oslo and Bergen
  • Integrated with bus network
  • Single ticket system for all transport
  • Regular service throughout the day
  • Night service on weekends

Air & Sea Travel

Domestic Flights

  • SAS and Norwegian main carriers
  • Widerøe for regional flights
  • Regular connections between cities
  • Airport express trains available
  • Book early for best prices

Ferries & Boats

  • Hurtigruten coastal service
  • Local ferries part of road network
  • Fjord sightseeing tours
  • Car ferries on major routes
  • Express boats between coastal cities

Car Travel

Car Rental

  • Major companies at airports
  • Electric cars widely available
  • Winter tires required (Oct-Apr)
  • Book in advance for better rates
  • International license accepted

Road Information

  • Right-hand driving
  • Toll roads common
  • Speed limits strictly enforced
  • AutoPASS for toll payment
  • Check road conditions in winter

Health & Insurance

Healthcare System

Emergency Services

  • Emergency number: 113
  • 24/7 emergency rooms in major cities
  • English-speaking medical staff
  • Ambulance service widely available
  • Air ambulance for remote areas

Medical Facilities

  • High-quality healthcare system
  • Modern hospitals in all major cities
  • Walk-in clinics available
  • Pharmacies widely distributed
  • After-hours medical services

Insurance Requirements

Required Coverage

  • Travel insurance mandatory
  • Medical evacuation coverage
  • Accident insurance
  • Liability coverage
  • Adventure sports coverage if applicable

EU/EEA Citizens

  • European Health Insurance Card valid
  • Additional travel insurance recommended
  • Check coverage before travel
  • Keep documentation accessible
  • Register card with local authorities

Health Tips

General Precautions

  • Tap water is safe to drink
  • Food hygiene standards are high
  • UV protection needed in summer
  • Winter clothing essential
  • Motion sickness pills for fjord cruises

Medications

  • Bring prescription documentation
  • Pack essential medications
  • Check medication restrictions
  • Keep medications in original packaging
  • Know generic names of medicines
